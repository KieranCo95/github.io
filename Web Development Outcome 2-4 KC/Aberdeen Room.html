Aberdeen Room

Embrace the rustic feel of the traveller in our Aberdeen room...

The Aberdeen Room embraces the feel of being a Scottish traveller of olden times. you could almost imagine the straw roof above the beams and ceiling plaster. The room features two single beds with spring/foam mattresses and standard pillows for that authentic rustic feel, without being uncomfortable.
