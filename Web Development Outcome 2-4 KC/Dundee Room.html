Dundee Room

Find comfort in our cosey Dundee room...

The Dundee Room is the definition of cosy. This room is perfect for an intimate romantic getaway as the window looks out onto the coast. On a clear night, you can see every star in the sky. In winter you can watch the northern lights from the comfort of your own room. This double bed has a spring/memory foam combo mattress and standard pillows.
